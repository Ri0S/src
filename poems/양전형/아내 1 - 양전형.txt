Because my wife is short, But she lifts up her feet from the ground in order to be hung to me. Among the surging crowd un a five days market, But she is hardly seen. How wide great her sitting place! How sturdy great her personal appearance! Every nook and corner in the home, Every crack in my children`s hearts, Every way of the sloppy doing to kill the night in my wandered mind, She holds me firmly. Really, Making full with a promise started at the first day, She is like a full moon which scatters the light all over the world. And blooming strongly in a windy cold weather following snow fall, She is like a pure narcissus.
