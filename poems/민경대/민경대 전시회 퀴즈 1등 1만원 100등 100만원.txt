A Michael Jordon comes along once in a decade, if not a generation. Through no choice of his own, he won the DNA lottery, making him unusually tall and able to leap tall buildings. The same is true for every professional athlete. Most of us can train all our lives and never run a 4.2 40 yard dash. Beauty is the same. Angelina Jolie, George Clooney, Heidi Klum, Jennifer Aniston, and Brad Pitt all won the DNA lottery. Most of us, on our best day, don't look as good as they do on their worst day. Winning the DNA lottery can bring you fame and money but it can't bring you real life. In fact, Jesus LOST the DNA lottery! The Bible says in Isaiah 53:2–3 (ESV) 2 For he grew up before him like a young plant, and like a root out of dry ground; he had no form or majesty that we should look at him, and no beauty that we should desire him. 3 He was despised and rejected by men; a man of sorrows, and acquainted with grief; and as one from whom men hide their faces he was despised, and we esteemed him not. Jesus is the most magnetic person in human history and it has nothing to do with giftedness, intelligence, looks, or strength. His popularity has been rooted in his ability, through God's strength, to live a life of total righteousness, humility, and servanthood. Hollywood, the NBA, and the NFL are only available to a few of us. The good news is that everyone can have the Jesus life.      나는 전시회를 한다.아번에는 나이 60세의 환갑기년 전시회다.영문학과 교수가 전시회를 하다보니 사실 시를 그림으로 표현한것이고 그리고 우리 인간만이 가지고 있는 언어 유전자 foxp2가 그림으로 바뀐것이다 나보다 훨씩 일찍 이 세상을 태어나 시를 쓴 ted hughes 는 sylvia plath와 결혼하고 그의 부인은 자살로 죽고 그도 1930-1998년 이란 기록울 두고 세상을 하직하였으나 그는 가고 시는 남았다  그의 시 The Thought-Fox 는 아주 내가 그린 그림속에 잘 표현되었다.이 시를 읽고 다음 생각을 해본 사람은 나의 그림을 이해할것이다 이번 퀴즈는  AS the title implies, Highes's poem is not about a literal fox.What does the figures of the thought-fox take?  민경대 전시회 46차례중 답을 작성하여 korea14@gmail.com 으로 보내주시면 1등부터 100등까지 dna lottery 하여 상품과 상금을 드림니다. 단 4월 1일 점수된 것은 무효입니다. 만우절말이기 때문입니다.  응모수수료: 1만원 상금 :꺼꾸로가는 상 100등 100만원 1등        1만원http://www.markedbyteachers.com/gcse/english/ted-hughes-the-thought-fox-is-an-effective-poem-on-both-a-literal-and-a-symbolic-level-would-you-agree.html    문제: Ted Hughes, the thought fox, is an effective poem on both a literal and a symbolic level.
