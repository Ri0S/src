I wrote this to help ease the pain I'm feeling watching his suffer. He is not just a Dad, he's my very best friend. He's never let me down. I believe in heaven and I know where he is going but it still doesn't make this any easier for me. I hope others facing a loss of a parent can read this and find comfort in knowing that life after life is real, heaven is real and one day we will all be together again.
